Background and Motivation

Harvard students send over 800,000 emails almost every day, reflecting information about their activities, classes, moods, interests, etc. There are over 7,000 lists hosted on the Harvard Computer Society's servers.

This is an incredible amount of data about Harvard students, with patterns in social interaction and student habits hidden within the millions of emails. Our motivation is to better understand the interactions within an email list so that students can tailor their emails to predicted responses and communicate more effectively.
